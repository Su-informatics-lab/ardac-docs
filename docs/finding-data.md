# Finding Data

Use the ARDaC Portal to discover available studies and data resources connected to AlcHepNet.

## Discovery Workflow

1. Open [portal.ardac.org](https://portal.ardac.org/).
2. Navigate to the portal discovery or data exploration area.
3. Search or filter for studies, cohorts, assays, specimens, or other available resource attributes.
4. Review the study details and metadata before deciding whether the data resource fits your research question.

## What to Look For

When evaluating a resource, review:

- study description and eligibility context
- data types and assay categories
- specimen or biosample information
- available clinical, demographic, laboratory, treatment, outcome, or omics metadata
- access notes or data availability guidance shown in the portal

## Next Steps

If a resource appears relevant, review the [Access](access.md) page and contact [ardac@iu.edu](mailto:ardac@iu.edu) with questions about availability or required approvals.
