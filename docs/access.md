# Access

The ARDaC Portal may include both public information and features that require sign-in or approved access. This starter page should be updated as ARDaC access policies and user workflows are finalized.

## Before Requesting Access

Prepare the information normally needed for a research data access request:

- your name and institutional affiliation
- your research role
- the study or data resource you want to use
- a brief description of your research purpose
- any required approvals or protocol details requested by the ARDaC team

## Sign In

Open the [ARDaC Portal](https://portal.ardac.org/) and use the available sign-in option when prompted.

!!! note
    Do not assume that sign-in alone grants access to controlled data. Confirm access requirements with the ARDaC team.

## Questions

Send access questions to [ardac@iu.edu](mailto:ardac@iu.edu).
